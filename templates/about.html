Purpose & Mission
At times, people —especially those who suffer from anxiety disorders— feel very anxious for seemingly no good reason. This feeling could be overwhelming, and if prolonged, often leads to depression. Navigating through and managing worries become difficult under the mental fog that accompanies heightened anxiety.

Through laying out worries, filtering them by whether they can be controlled, and accepting the uncontrollable to be left with only the actionable, laid out's raison d'être is to help the user manage their anxiety a bit better.

While it's largely impossible to cure anxiety solely using this tool, what laid out aims to achieve is merely to help lift some of the mental fog off of the user's mind which in turn would empower them to address the root causes of their anxiety and work towards long-term emotional well-being.
Motivation
The motivation for laid out was two-fold at the beginning; to create a tool to manage my anxiety, and by doing that, learn how to code. It was clear from the get-go that if the tool ended up being actually useful, many other people like me could also benefit from it. The prospect of helping people struggling with anxiety made laid out a passion project for me.
